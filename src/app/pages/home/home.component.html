Welcome to Smart Thermostat

Introducing our cutting-edge home assistant add-on that will revolutionize the way you control your smart thermostat.
Powered by advanced artificial intelligence,
this innovative solution seamlessly integrates with your existing smart home system,
providing you with unparalleled convenience and comfort. How does it work? Start by adding sensors and assigning a type.
After this the system will automatically start learning your habits and preferences
and it will create automation('s) bases on these.

After the automation's are created you need to grade them and the system will
add the accepted automation('s) to Home Assistant.
